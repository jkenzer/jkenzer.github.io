# Observing vs Thinking

You can't simultaneously be in thinking mode and observing mode. So one way to get out of the rut of negative thoughts is to switch to observing mode. 

Besides for mindfully switching, activities that cause you to focus can work as well: reading, coding, even watching a compelling show or movie.
