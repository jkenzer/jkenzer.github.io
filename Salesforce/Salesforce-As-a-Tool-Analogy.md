# Salesforce as a tool

## Admin, consultant, or developer limitations
You can give someone an excellent high-quality hammer. Good heft, nice grip and the perfect fit. However, if they hit their thumb multiple times and bend the nail as they are pounding it in, they will say it's not good.

You can give that same hammer to someone else and they could build award-winning furniture or a home for someone who needs a home.

It's the same hammer. A tool needs to not only be used properly but skillfully.

## Not setting the users up for success
The other factor is sometimes the hammer is given to someone with the wrong size nail for the job or they are asked to hammer the nail into a cinder block instead of wood. Success with the hammer is going to be very difficult no matter how good the hammer is, if the pieces around it aren't aligned.
